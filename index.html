About Me

Cohen Gamboa is a Chicago based audiophile-industry professional. He began his career in Minneapolis at the Needle Doctor, a small audiophile retailer. He currently works as the Merchandiser for Music Direct, an audiophile distrubutor and retailer of high-end audiophile components and vinyl. He also works in affiliation with Mobile Fidelity Sound Lab, Mofi Electronics, and Mofi Distribution.
The Music Direct family of brands are an essential group doing their part to keep high quality sound reproduction available to the masses. As a lover of many styles of music, he believes in the power of music to bring us closer together.
